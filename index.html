Design Manifesto

Author: Layla Quinones

My Aesthetic

With this design manifesto I decided to choose a light theme because I wanted users to have a joyful and light experience. The title is on the left hand side and the image is on the right hand side to guide the viewers eye from top to bottom and left to right on the page. This text is placed on a light blue card so that the reader understands that this information is important, but does not clutter the view.

White Space

I put every element of this website in a container so that there are nice margins and white space at the ends. This whitespace also functions as a way to seperate important information on this site and, to help the reader distinguish which information is more important. Below I used white space to seperate cards and butons with different information. I sperated groups of information using different rows from Materialize. These have larger spaces between rows to seperate groups of information. Below I centered elements and spaced them out evenly to keep breathing room consistant and visually appealing.
